About FinWealth

The pandemic has caused a financial strain on many individuals. The student loan debt continues to increase with no clear solution and many are struggling to provide and care for themselves and others under this financial stress. Finwealth gives users an incentive to save, invest, and help them become financially responsible by rewarding them with gift cards, coupons, etc. We help them discover ways to invest safely by finding them investments that fit their financial profile/portfolio. We also give them budgeting tips based on the progress they have made.
